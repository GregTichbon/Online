Log in help

If you have already registered with the Whanganui District Council through this online system please enter your email address or user name and password to continue.

If you have not already registered with the Whanganui District Council through this online system please click on the "Register" link at the bottom of the form.

If you have forgotten your email address and user name or password please click on the "Log in assistance" link at the bottom of the form.

You can use either your email address OR user name, either will grant you access to your online account.
