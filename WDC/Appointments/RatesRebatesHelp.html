Rates Rebate Appointment Scheduler

Making things easy

Rates rebate applications must be completed in person with a member of the Whanganui District Council staff.

Making an appointment will ensure your application is processed promptly and will avoid any undue delays to you.

If you would like to make an appointment please click on the green button in the time slot that suits you, appointments are 15 minutes.

Identify your property using the search at the top of the form, you may search by "Property Address", "Property Number", or "Assessment Number". Enter your contact information and "Submit" the form.

You will receive an automatically generated email confirming your appointment.
This email will provide a link to allow you to change details, including the date and time, or to cancel your appointment.

If you are unsure as to your eligibility for a Rates Rebate you can use the Rates Rebate Estimate Calculator.
