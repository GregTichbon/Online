Please use this form as your initial enquiry for booking one of the spaces within the Whanganui Venues & Events portfolio.

We understand that you may be in the initial stages of your event planning, so please provide us with as much information as you can in order for us to offer you the best options for your event.

Upon completion of the form, one of our dedicated Event Coordinators will get back to you within 48 hours.

If you have any issues filling out this form please email events@whanganui.govt.nz
