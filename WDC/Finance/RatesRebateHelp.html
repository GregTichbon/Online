Rates Rebate

Estimate Calculator

You can use this calculator to see if you can get a rates rebate and estimate how much you might get.

The rates shown are the annual rates less the prompt payment discount, as Rates Rebates are calculated on these.

The calculation is an estimate only.

The Horizons rates were accurate as as at 13 July 2018.

Please identify your property using the search at the top of the form, you may search by "Property Address", "Property Number", or "Assessment Number"

Enter your annual gross income (as at 31 March 2018) and number of dependant children and click on the "Calculate" button.
