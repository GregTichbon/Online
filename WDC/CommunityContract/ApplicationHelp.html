You can SAVE and SUBMIT your application.

SAVE - You can do this as often as you like. No validation will occur. You can continue to work on your application.

SUBMIT - You can also do this as often as you like, but validation will occur, an email copy of your application will be sent to you and you will be redirected to the menu page

The final question allows you to finalise your application.  Once the application is finalised no amendments will be allowed. The application must be finalised prior to the closing date to be considered by Council. This option is only allowed on submit and you will be asked to verify the answer.

The following are a list of the questions that are required to be answered.

  • Application Details
    • Group's reference number
    • What type of funding are you seeking?
    • Name of the project / activity
    • Describe the project / activity
    • Who, how many and how will people benefit?
    • What are the planned outcomes and how will they be measured?
    • Please tell us how your application relates to the following five (5) goals in our "Whanganui: Leading Edge" vision.  More information on the vision is available here   (Please enter N/A if your application does not relate to a particular goal)
      • Deeply United - We are a place resounding with community spirit, we support each other, work in partnership and are pulling in the same direction.
      • Globally Connected - We are outward looking, we are connected in the widest sense through our network infrastructure, digital capacity, expansive ideas and external relationships.
      • Creative Smarts - We are innovative, entrepreneurial, go getters, we actively attract industry, support start ups.
      • Flowing with Richness - We draw strength from the river and this sustains and shapes us. It feels positive here and there is a lot going on. Our wealth is abundant and we take a broad view of what this means. We play on our strengths and make our own opportunities - trumpetting our unique identity through placemaking that flows from the mountain to the river to the coast.
      • Works for Everyone - We have boundless opportunities and are truly a place of choice for all. There is a diversity to what we offer and people are able to make a conscious decision to come here and stay
    • How will this project fit within the Safer Whanganui Framework?
    • Who and how do you collaborate with others?
    • Please upload any other information that may aid your application
