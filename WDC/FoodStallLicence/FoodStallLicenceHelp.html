To sell food on Whanganui District Council land you will need a permit under the Public Places, Parks and Reserves Bylaw 2016.

Allow 5 working days for Whanganui District Council to process this application and to issue a licence.

If you have any questions please contact the Food Safety Officers at the Whanganui District Council email wdc@wanganui.govt.nz or ph: 06 3490001.
