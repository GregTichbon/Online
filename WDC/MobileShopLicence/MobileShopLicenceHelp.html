Mobile Shop Licence Application

Complete each field that is applicable.  Provide as much information as possible to assist council in processing your application..

  • The following information is required:
    • The name of your business
    • Whether you intend to operate within the whole of the Whanganui District or not.
    • If you have stated that you will NOT be operating within the whole of the Whanganui District, the location(s) you will be operating are required.  (You can draw this using an online map associated with this form)
    • If you will be operating in front of a premise, the business name, owners, phone numbers, and confirmation that you have received permission from the owners to operate.

 

 If an application fee is applicable an invoice will be emailed to you, (processing of your application will not begin until payment has been received).  From payment being received your application will take up to 5 working days to process.
