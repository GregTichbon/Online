Fire Safety Check and Smoke Alarm
Referral Form

  Please fill in as much information as possible so that the Fire Service can make contact with the household contact people to arrange a time to visit.

Consent from the residence of the property must be obtained prior to completing this application form.

A copy of this information will be emailed to the Whanganui Fire Service. 

This information will also be forwarded to those others for whom email addresses have been provided. 

Where consent is granted, the information entered will be recorded in the Civil Defence emergency database.

Please note that this referral does not guarantee any free smoke alarms.  The recommended smoke alarm is a 10 year photoelectric one.

If a question is not relevant please just enter N/A (not applicaple) or something similar.
