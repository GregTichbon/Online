Location Selection Map

Please enter a short description of the location.

To identify the area you can chose the "Map" or "Satelite" view.

Type an address into the "Search Box" to take you to the approximate location.

Zoom in or out to better view the area.  This can be done with the "+" and "-" buttons towards the bottom right of the window, or with a scroll wheel on your mouse if you have one.

You can also move the map around by clicking and holding the left mouse button and dragging the map.

Click on the map to create as many markers as required to identify the boundary of the area you are defining.  To "close" the boundary, click on the first marker (the mouse pointer will change from an open hand to a pointing finger.

If you are satisified with the area you have selected click on the "Select" button at the bottom right of the window.

If you would like to redraw the area click on the "Reset" button at the bottom left of the window.

Only one area can be drawn at a time.
