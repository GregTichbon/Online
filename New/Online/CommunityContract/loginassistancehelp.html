Login Assistance

If you do not have a record of the username and/or password please choose the appropriate option and provide the details requested.

If you don't have a record of the username, you will be requested to enter the email address that was last recorded. An email will be sent to this address with your username.

If you don't have a record of the password, you will be requested to enter the username. An email will be sent to the email address that was last recorded with a link to reset your password.

If you are still having difficulty accessing your group, please contact Lauren Tamehana at the Whanganui District Council (06) 349-0001.
